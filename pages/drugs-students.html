Datavisualization

Students and drugs usage

Student representation HvA

Data Project 2020

Collecting, analyzing and representating datasets.

Keywords

  • Design research
  • Prototyping

Data and personal information obtained from fellow students to find insights to make interesting and meaningful combinations by combining several datasets. By asking critical questions I was able to find if there is a similiarity of drug use with students that live more closeby the city than farther away from the city.
With this information I created a visually appealing Datavisualization that displays the data correctly and also give the reader a motivation to keep on reading. Using visualization methods to show qualitative data and quantitative data respectively.

Analyzing the dataset

The dataset consisted out of a set of questions which totals of 20 per student, to create an interesting combination I checked the questions that involved drug use. Because drugs was the topic of the news a few times I wanted to investigate if there are factors which increases drug use.
The questions I was interested in was the level of study these students did , the location and how high they rate their happiness. For these questions I looked for a correlation between the kind of drug and the above questions. I created a small visualization, whenever I get a positive result between the two I added them to the final product.

Representing the dataset

The dataset is visualized in Adobe Xd, I made use of a proportional symbol map combined with a bar chart to show the quantitative values that are linked to a location. Most of the bar charts used in my visualization other than the one used in the map are used to give a comparison of how much debt students have and if there is a correlation to drug use and the amount of study debt.
I made the choice to use barcharts and a location map based visualization to engage the user and show the impact of drug use in amsterdam in this case. Below you can find the full working prototype.
